LUNGTI

Providing IoT service through Augmented and Virtual Reality Tour with Navigation in Manila Zoo.


The project will be a valuable contribution to the field of education and tourism. It offers visitors a fresh and captivating approach to discovering and learning about zoo animals and their habitats, through the integration of IoT. By using a QR reader to access trivia-based questions related to the animals and their habitats, visitors can test their knowledge and learn new information. The augmented reality navigation system within the Manila Zoo provides a seamless navigational experience, making it easier for visitors to navigate the zoo and find their desired destination. With the use of cutting-edge AR/VR technology, the initiative aims to deliver an exceptional and immersive experience that will ultimately enrich the overall visit to the Manila Zoo for users.

LEXICON

Word Game Using Unity


This project is made to help students remember and learn more academic words while having fun. The game is both a way to enjoy playing and to learn something useful. Each level is designed to give players new words to learn and keep them interested.

This project is great for grade school and high school students. It can also help teachers by being a tool that adds to regular classroom lessons. By turning learning into a game, this project helps students remember words better and improve their thinking and problem-solving skills. It mixes fun and learning, making it good for students of any age.
